What TITAN Does

Every Transport Function.
One Unified Platform.

From travel request to claims reimbursement — TITAN digitalizes the entire institutional transportation lifecycle.

📋
Digital Travel Authorization
Submit travel requests digitally. Multi-tier approval routing from department head to Chancellor — fully traceable and timestamped.
📍
Real-Time GPS Fleet Monitoring
Live vehicle tracking with geofencing alerts. Instantly know when a vehicle deviates from its authorized route.
🚗
Smart Fleet Scheduling
Algorithmic vehicle and driver assignment. Prevents double-booking and ensures fair, equitable driver workload distribution.
⛽
Automated Fuel Monitoring
Real-time diesel and gasoline prices auto-scraped from DOE. Accurate trip cost estimates linked to claims management.
💰
Claims Management
Digital submission of per diems, toll fees, and incidentals. Automated computation reduces manual errors in reimbursements.
📊
Compliance Reports & Analytics
Auto-generated monthly reports for institutional auditing. Vehicle utilization, fuel summaries, and driver performance dashboards.
Process Flow

From Request to Dispatch.

1
Submit Request
Faculty or staff files a digital travel request with memo and authority-to-travel document
2
Multi-Tier Approval
Request routes automatically from Department Head → Dean → Chancellor for authorization
3
Vehicle Dispatched
GSO assigns vehicle and driver algorithmically. Digital trip ticket generated automatically
4
Claims & Reports
Driver submits post-trip data. Personnel files claims. Reports auto-generated for compliance
